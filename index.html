RULES

Competitors can participate in a team or as individuals, there is no limit for how many people can be on a team. Extra points will be given for teams as performing together requires more harmony. Contests can either send a voice recording or a video, but the audio should be clear. Nasheed cannot exceed 4 minutes. No music or daff is allowed. Nasheed/naat must be islamic, and lyrics must be appropriate with no reference of shirk. The nasheed/naat can be done in any language.

10% Fluency according to the lyrics
60% Beauty of voice & melody
10% Pronunciation
